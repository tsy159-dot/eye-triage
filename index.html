Eye Casualty Triage Assistant

Step-by-step guide for staff
ℹ️ Decision support only. This follows local emergency-eye triage guidance, but your clinical judgement and senior / on-call advice always take priority. Out of hours, the on-call eye doctor decides priority. It does not give a diagnosis.

What the colours mean

How soon the patient needs to be seen.

Red — Emergency
See now / same session. Out of hours: phone the on-call eye team.
Orange — Within 24 hours
Arrange to be seen in eye casualty within a day.
Amber — Within 3 days
Out of hours may wait overnight / weekend at on-call discretion.
Blue — Fast track / within a few days
Dedicated clinic pathway (e.g. retina / macula imaging).
Grey — Not an eye-casualty emergency
Redirect (optician / pharmacy / GP / other), as shown.
